About Us

Welcome to Scania Trans! We are a dedicated team of professionals specializing in Scania truck diagnostics and repairs. Our mission is to provide top-notch services to keep your Scania trucks running smoothly and efficiently.

Our Story

Founded in 1979, Scania Trans started with a small workshop and a big dream. Our passion for Scania trucks and our commitment to quality service has driven us to expand our operations and continually improve our offerings.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced mechanics, technicians, and support staff who share a common goal: delivering the best possible service to our customers. We are proud to have built a reputation for reliability and excellence.

Our Values

  • Quality: We ensure that every job we undertake is done to the highest standards.
  • Integrity: We believe in honest, transparent communication with our customers.
  • Innovation: We stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and methods in truck diagnostics and repair.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers' needs and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
